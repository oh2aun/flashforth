marker -trig9
: Pi $0fdb $4049 ;   \ Most precise Pi
: d2r Pi 180.0e f/ ;
: r2d 180.0e Pi f/ ;
: TRIG9
		d2r f* fsin
		d2r f* fcos
		d2r f* ftan
		fatan r2d f*
		facos r2d f*
		fasin r2d f*
;

\   not with single precision, however :)
